Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and services

What's included in the basic service package?

Our basic package includes one standard service (photography, sound recording, dubbing, audio mixing, video editing, or color grading) for projects up to 5 minutes in length. You'll receive 2 rounds of revisions and delivery within 7 business days.

How do I know which package is right for me?

The right package depends on your project scope, timeline, and budget. Basic is ideal for simple, single-service projects, Standard works well for mid-sized projects requiring multiple services, and Premium is best for complex, full-production projects with tight deadlines. Still unsure? Contact us for a personalized recommendation.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations, educational institutions, and student projects. Please contact us with details about your organization and project for more information.

What happens if I need revisions beyond what's included?

Additional revisions beyond your package allowance are billed at $100 per hour. We'll always notify you before incurring any extra charges and provide an estimate of the time required for your requested changes.

Is there a cancellation fee?

Cancellations made more than 7 days before the project start date receive a full refund minus a 10% administrative fee. Cancellations between 2-7 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 48 hours of the project start are non-refundable.

How do monthly retainers work?

Monthly retainers provide reserved capacity for recurring production needs. You'll sign a 3-month minimum agreement and pay monthly in advance. Unused capacity doesn't roll over to the next month, but you'll always have priority scheduling for your allocated projects.
